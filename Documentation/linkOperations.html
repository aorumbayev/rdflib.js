Using linkOperation

The linkOperation method provides a "complete" delete and copy. In other words, it deletes or copies a resource or container AND any .acl or .meta files found in the resource's Link header. The method operates on a single resource at a time - use recursiveCopy or recursiveDelete to operate on an entire tree.

fetcher.linkOperation( method, source, destination)

The method parameter should be one of "DELETE", "COPY", or "GET". The second parameter should be the resource to delete or copy or to get the link headers from. The third parameter, used only with "COPY" is the destination to copy to. The second and third parameters may be a string representation of a URI or an rdflib NamedNode.
